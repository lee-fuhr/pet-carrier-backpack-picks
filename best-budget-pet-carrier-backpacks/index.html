Pet carrier backpacks have become a go‑to for traveling with your furry friend without the bulk of a traditional crate. A good budget model saves you money without skimping on ventilation, safety, or comfort for both you and your pet. Many now include padded interiors, multiple entry points, and adjustable straps, features once reserved for pricier options. When choosing, focus on the carrier’s ventilation (mesh panels are essential), secure zippers and clips, and a sturdy base that won’t sag. Also consider your pet’s size and temperament: a calm cat may enjoy a bubble window, while a wiggly small dog is safer in a sling that keeps them close.
